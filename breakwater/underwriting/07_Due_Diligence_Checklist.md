# Breakwater Capital Partners
## Due Diligence Checklist *(Acquisition Memorandum — Appendix G)*

*The confirmatory backbone that turns a memo's conditions precedent into verified fact. One diligence miss can turn a 27% spread into a loss — this checklist exists to prevent that. Every item has an **owner** and a **status**; the deal does not close until all gating items clear.*

---

## How to use

- **Statuses:** ☐ Open · ◑ In progress · ☑ Cleared · ⚑ Issue (escalate) · N/A
- **Gating (G) items** must be ☑ before close. A ⚑ on any gating item halts the deal until resolved, repriced, or restructured.
- Run in parallel with the underwriting model and the acquisition memo; update the memo's §11 conditions precedent as items clear.
- **Owners:** RR = Richard Rosa / principal · TITLE = in-house title · LEGAL = in-house counsel · DEV = development oversight · GC = build partner · ARCH = architecture partner · DSGN = design partner · BKR = brokerage.
