# Breakwater Capital Partners
## Acquisition Memorandum — TEMPLATE

> **What this is.** The per-deal investment document. It turns a screened, modeled opportunity into a specific "yes" — for the internal investment committee (IC) first, then for the capital partner. One memo per asset.
>
> **Drafting rules.**
> - *Lead with risk, then opportunity.* The reader should believe you have already tried to kill the deal.
> - *Every number traces to a source* — the underwriting model, a comp, a bid, a term sheet. No unsourced figures.
> - *Be specific.* "Liens resolvable" is not a sentence; "$340K mechanic's lien from [GC], resolvable via [path]" is.
> - *Italic bracketed text `[like this]` is guidance — delete it.* Replace every `‹placeholder›`.
> - Target length: 6–10 pages. If it's longer, you're hiding the decision.
