The Thesis

Distress is how we source. Completion is what we sell.

Higher rates, cost inflation, and over-leveraged developers have stranded luxury waterfront projects across Fort Lauderdale and South Florida. Lenders, receivers, and estates need these assets off their books — but few buyers can resolve the liens, restructure the debt, and finish the construction. That gap is the opportunity, and it is mispriced.

We are not a discount buyer of broken projects. We are the certain-execution counterparty a lender, receiver, or estate calls when a half-built waterfront asset has become a problem they cannot solve.

Our Edge

Anyone can buy cheap. Almost no one can finish.

Most distressed buyers fail because completion demands legal, title, debt, brokerage, construction, and design capability under one roof. Breakwater is totally vertical — controlling, or partnering to control, every link from the note to the finished, furnished home.
