Florida is one of the most-targeted states for home title theft — criminals forge a deed to your home, then sell it or borrow against it. Here's how to close the gap on the property you already own.

The gap: For 20+ years, the standard owner's title policy you got at closing has excluded forgery that happens after your closing (Exclusion 3(d)) — exactly how title theft works.
The fix: The new ALTA 49 / 49.1 endorsements (2025) add real, post-closing forgery coverage to your owner's policy. DeedGuard wraps that coverage in active protection — and we issue it through Old Republic Title.
