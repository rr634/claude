The threat

Your Florida home's title is a public document — and that's the problem.

Florida's vacant lots, second homes, and snowbird properties are among the nation's most-targeted. Deeds are filed at the county, and a fraudster only needs to forge a transfer, file it, and on paper your home now belongs to someone else. They borrow against it, sell it, or evict tenants — and you don't find out until the damage is done. For 20+ years the standard owner's title policy you bought at closing excluded this exact fraud (Exclusion 3(d)).

DeedGuard closes that gap end to end — we deter the fraud, watch the record, add real forgery insurance, and put attorneys on it if it ever happens.

Questions

What you should know.

Doesn't my title insurance already cover this?
The standard ALTA 2021 owner's policy only covers forgery that happened before your policy date — for 20+ years it has excluded post-closing forgery (Exclusion 3(d)). That's the gap home title theft exploits. The new ALTA 49 / 49.1 endorsements (released 2025) close it, and DeedGuard adds that endorsement to your policy — then layers monitoring, a recorded deterrent, and attorney-led restoration on top.
What is the ALTA 49 / 49.1 endorsement?
They're new American Land Title Association endorsements for post-closing deed/mortgage forgery. ALTA 49 attaches to a new owner's policy at closing; ALTA 49.1 is added to an existing owner's policy. They cover losses from a forged deed or mortgage recorded after the endorsement date that you had no knowledge of. DeedGuard issues these through Old Republic Title.
How do I get the ALTA 49 / 49.1 coverage added?
Two clean paths: if you already hold an Old Republic owner's title policy, we add the ALTA 49.1 endorsement to it; if your home doesn't have an eligible owner's policy yet, we issue a new owner's policy with ALTA 49. It's available on 1–4 family homes owned by an individual or living trust, and we handle the paperwork as an Old Republic Title agent. Coverage begins at the endorsement date.
Is DeedGuard actual insurance, or just monitoring?
Both — and more. Most "title lock" services only watch the record and send an alert; the financial loss stays with you. DeedGuard pairs monitoring with a recorded deterrent and a real title-insurance endorsement (ALTA 49/49.1 via Old Republic Title) that pays to clear the fraud and restore clean title.
What does the coverage pay for?
The endorsement covers loss from a forged deed or mortgage recorded against your home after closing, including the cost to clear the public record — subject to the policy's terms, conditions, exclusions, and limits. Our attorney-led team manages the restoration, with the policy covering the cost.
Can DeedGuard physically stop a fraudulent filing?
No service can prevent a criminal from submitting a document to the county. Our recorded protection notice puts a documented ownership claim in your chain of title so title companies and lenders apply extra scrutiny, our monitoring detects a bad filing almost immediately, and the endorsement plus our attorneys make you whole — so fraud is deterred, caught in hours or days, and covered, not discovered months later in a foreclosure notice.
What happens when you detect something suspicious?
You get an instant alert by email (and text on Defend and Portfolio plans) with details of exactly what was recorded. On attorney-backed plans, our real estate legal team helps you verify whether it's legitimate and, if not, dispute the filing and notify the county recorder.
How do you monitor my property?
We match your address to its parcel in the official county land records and snapshot the current, legitimate deed and title. From then on, every new document recorded against that parcel is checked against your baseline and scored for risk.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. We use your address only to look up public land records, and your contact details only to send your alerts. We never sell your data. Property records themselves are already public — we simply watch them for you.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. There are no contracts. Cancel from your dashboard whenever you like and monitoring stops at the end of your billing period.
